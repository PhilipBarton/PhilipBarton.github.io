Strong Coffee, Strong Roots About Our Cafe

Philip Barton is a multi-faceted music industry professional with a myriad of skills in the music industry. Whether it's graphic design, songwriting, journalism or social media marketing, he's your man. Dedicated to creating a simple, polished product that will work for any client.

He admires simple design, crisp lines, and simple geometric shapes. Philip was inspired by 80s color palates, 60s typography, and industrial design. His songwriting is much in the same vein; simplicity is key. It's about getting the idea across with the simplest message in the fewest words. His social media marketing upon building a client's fan base by better understanding community interaction and targeting the fan base the client wants. As a journalist, Philip runs Newfoundland Noise, a music magazine dedicated to covering Newfoundland music and asking questions other magazines don't ask. First and foremost is a cheerleader for all his client's cause when they succeed he succeeds.
